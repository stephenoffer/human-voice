# Q3 Onboarding Review

Onboarding is the foundation of our retention numbers, and right now we are only scratching the surface of what it could do. The current flow gives users the building blocks, but it leaves the hardest steps as uncharted waters.

Self-serve activation is a double-edged sword. It lowers our support cost, which is the backbone of our margin, but it also means a confused user has no one to ask. The drop-off we see at the workspace-setup step is just the tip of the iceberg; behind it sits a much larger group who finish setup and still never return.

Account provisioning is the cornerstone of the whole experience, and it remains the lifeblood of trial conversion. Today it has too many moving parts. Each integration is one more piece of the puzzle a new admin has to assemble alone, and the landscape of connectors we support keeps growing without a guided path through it.

Fixing the setup step would be a gateway to better week-one numbers and a springboard for the upsell motion the sales team has been asking for.
