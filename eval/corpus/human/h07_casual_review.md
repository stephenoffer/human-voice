# Three weeks with the cheap label printer

I bought the Munbyn thermal printer because the Brother one cost four times as much and I print maybe ten labels a week. Here's the honest verdict: for ten labels a week, the cheap one is fine.

Setup was annoying. The driver page is in broken English and the Mac install needed a reboot it didn't warn me about. Once it worked, it worked. Labels come out crisp, the thermal paper hasn't faded on the packages sitting in my hallway, and I haven't touched ink because there isn't any.

The catch is the rolls. The printer is cheap so they sell you the paper forever. Off-brand rolls jam about one time in twenty. I went back to the official ones and the jamming stopped, which I suspect was the plan all along.
