# Why we're raising the Team plan to $18

We're moving the Team plan from $12 to $18 per seat in March. I want to explain the reasoning before the email goes out, because some of you will field the questions.

The short version: we underpriced it two years ago and never corrected. At $12 we were charging less than three of our four closest competitors, and our gross margin on Team accounts had drifted to 61%, below the 70% the board signed off on. We either raise the price or cut the support those accounts get, and cutting support is how you lose the accounts.

So we raise.

Existing customers keep $12 for twelve months. New signups pay $18 starting March 1. We modeled the churn from the increase at somewhere between 4% and 7% of Team seats, and even at the high end the revenue per remaining seat more than covers it.

What I'm less sure about is timing. We're shipping the new audit-log feature in February, and I'd rather the price change land after customers have seen it, not before. If February slips, we should hold the increase a month. I'd rather be late than look greedy.
