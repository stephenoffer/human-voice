# The day the office plant tried to kill the server

We kept a fern on top of the rack. It was a joke at first, a green thing in a room of beige metal. Someone watered it on a Friday and went home.

The pot had no drainage tray. Water seeped through the bottom, ran down the side of the rack, and found the one switch whose cover had been left off during a maintenance job two weeks earlier. Monday morning, half the floor's network was down and the on-call engineer spent two hours convinced it was a routing bug.

The fern is now on a shelf across the room, in a pot the size of a bucket, with a saucer. We did not fire anyone. We did write "no liquids above the rack" on the door in marker, and it is still there, and I think about it every time I bring coffee anywhere near a computer.
