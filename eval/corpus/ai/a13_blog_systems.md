# Why Great Teams Just Work

What makes a team great? Why do some groups ship while others stall? How do the best ones keep their momentum? These are the questions that matter.

Nobody designed the way your team makes decisions. People tend to assume structure emerges on its own. Humans are wired to follow the path of least resistance, and so the culture shifts without anyone noticing.

Here's the uncomfortable truth: the process tells you what it wants. A complaint becomes a fix. The conversation moves toward consensus. The data tells us where the friction lives, and the market rewards the teams that listen.

The implications are significant. The reasons are structural. This is genuinely hard.

So what do you do? You watch. You listen. You adjust. That's it. That's the whole playbook.
