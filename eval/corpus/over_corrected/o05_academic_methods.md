this section outlines the methodology. honestly? the sampling is the load-bearing choice here, ngl.

we recruited 240 participants through stratified random sampling. idk why prior work skipped stratification tbh, it matters.

the design is a between-subjects experiment. two conditions. control and treatment.

measures were collected at three time points. baseline. post-intervention. four week follow-up, fr.

the dependent variable is task accuracy. it's giving a clean signal once we control for prior experience. no notes.

we preregistered the hypotheses. lol at reviewers who still ask if we p-hacked. read the OSF link.

attrition was 11 percent, balanced across conditions. that's growth over the field's usual dropout, smh.

analysis uses mixed effects models with participant as a random intercept. standard. defensible.

limitations are discussed below. the sample skews young, so generalization is bounded.
