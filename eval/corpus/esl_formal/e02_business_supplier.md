# Recommendation: switch our packaging supplier

I recommend that we end the contract with Meridian Pack and move to Toldex before the September renewal. This is not a small decision, and I have thought about it carefully.

Meridian has raised prices three times in eighteen months. The last increase, in March, was 11 percent, and they gave us only two weeks of notice. Their quality has also slipped. In Q1 we received two pallets where the corrugated board was below the agreed thickness, and we had to repack 600 units by hand. The warehouse team was not happy.

Toldex quoted us 8 percent below Meridian's current rate for the same specification. I visited their plant near Rotterdam in April. The line was clean, the staff answered my technical questions without hesitation, and they offered a 90-day trial with no penalty if we walk away.

There is one risk. Toldex has never supplied a customer of our volume before, so the first months will need close watching. I propose we move 40 percent of orders first, keep Meridian as backup until December, then switch fully if the trial holds. The savings alone are worth roughly 70 thousand euros a year.
